How has technology affected our social life?

August 2, 2019

Over few decades, technology has been developing rapidly. We can even analyze drastic changes on our soical lifestyles due to technological advancement if we simply compare our childhood where we used to play on fields and now where online game has been most popular. Meanwhile, it has been affecting almost every aspect of our social life.

Technology has made our world smaller. Now we are able to say that the world is a virtual village which has narrowed social and physical gap among people. For example, you can easily connect with people around the globe using social medias on Internet. Similarly, there has been massive improvement on the means we travel. In the meantime, we can visit across country or ocean with planes, trains and ships. Also it has significantly improved medical technologies on various fields like biotechnology, pharmaceuticals and artificial organ transplantation. Now life expectancy of people has increased globally because of quick diagnosis and treatment of diseases with the use of advanced technologies.

What are the impacts of technology on professional environment?

May 20, 2018

Since technological revolution, there has been a drastic change on working environment. Earlier when paid jobs where meant for men, now there has been plenty opportunities for women too as working method has been quite simpler.

Technology has upgraded the working method across every industry. It has improved tedious and manual working methods to productive and automatic system. Also most of the jobs now are based on internet. Employees can work in a productive workspace and collaborate as an online team to perform any task at global level. People can interact with each other through internet where different public forums such as stack overflow are helping people to share their doubts and knowledge. Most importantly, creative and logical ideas have overtaken physical load of work.

With these impacts of technology on working mechanism, it has helped a lot for gender equality. Also people have been smarter and can collaborate with world to accomplish any task.
